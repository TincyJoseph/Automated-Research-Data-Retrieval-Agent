KERALA PLACES TO VISIT – ATTRACTIONS, HOTELS, AND BUDGETS

1) VARKALA BEACH
Main Attractions:
- Cliffs overlooking Arabian Sea
- Sunset views, beaches, water sports
- Janardana Swami Temple nearby 
Nearby Hotels (Example / Synthetic):
- Cliff View Resort (Mid) – close to beach
- Sea Breeze Guesthouse (Budget)
- Ocean Delight Suites (Premium)
Typical Budget Guidance:
- Budget: ₹800–₹1,500/night
- Mid-range: ₹2,000–₹4,000/night
- Premium: ₹5,000+
